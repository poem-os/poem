## Overview

Extracts "breakout moments" from video transcript - self-contained divergences from main sequential content (tangents, demos, emergent learnings). JSON output with position tracking. **New technique** (no Section 1 equivalent).

**Purpose**: Identify repurposable content (e.g., breakouts → shorts, demos → standalone tutorials).

---

## Key Pattern: Signal Phrase Detection

**Signal Phrases**: "Let me just...", "I'm going to open a new conversation...", "Let me show you...", "Here's what happened...", "Actually, let me...", "Side note...", "One thing I noticed..."

These indicate tangents or emergent content that could stand alone.

---

## Use Case: Content Repurposing

Breakout moments are candidates for:
- YouTube Shorts
- Standalone tutorial videos
- Social media clips
- "Behind the scenes" content
