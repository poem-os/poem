## Key Pattern: Structural Segmentation

**6 Segment Types**:
- `hook`: Opening line(s) to grab attention
- `promise`: What viewer will learn/see
- `seriesContext`: Reference to being part of series
- `signOff`: Transition phrase (e.g., "I'm AppyDave, let's get into it")
- `chapterMention`: Reference to chapter links
- `demoPreview`: Brief showing of end result

**Note**: "Extract actual text, not a paraphrase" - verbatim extraction.
