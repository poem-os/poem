## Overview

Extracts ALL calls-to-action (CTAs) from video transcript with position tracking (intro, early, middle, late, outro). JSON output with items array. Evolved version of Section 1's find-video-cta.

**Purpose**: Map CTA distribution across video for optimization (e.g., "Are CTAs too concentrated in outro?").

**Evolution**: Section 1-7 (find-video-cta) extracted specific CTAs. This extracts ALL CTAs with positions.

---

## Key Pattern: Position-Based CTA Mapping

**5 Positions**: intro | early | middle | late | outro

Enables analysis: "Which third of video has most CTAs?" "Are CTAs front-loaded or back-loaded?"
